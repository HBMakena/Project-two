Get to know your Akan Name

An Akan name is a name derivered fron Ghanian culture.In Ghana, children are given their first names as a
"day name" which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

All you have to do is fill in your birthday and your gender to know your Akan Name.
For the male; Kwasi, Kwadwo, Kwabena, Kwaku, Yaw, Kofi and Kwame.
For the female; Akosua, Adwoa, Abenaa, Akua, Yaa, Afua and Ama.
